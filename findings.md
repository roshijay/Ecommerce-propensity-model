## Business Recommendations

1. Target the 4,109 high-propensity customers (score 4-5) with 
   re-engagement campaigns, they show all five signals of repeat purchase behavior.

2. Prioritize inventory and promotions in home/lifestyle categories 
   (bed/bath, home decor, garden tools),  these drive 15-25% repeat 
   rates vs 3-6% for electronics.

3. Expand voucher programs strategically,  voucher users return at 7x 
   the rate of credit card users, though the relationship is bidirectional. 

4. Delivery promise padding is excessive,  orders arrive 11 days early 
   on average, which could be reframed as a positive surprise for customers. 

5. Investigate the 1,386 orders with null product categories, 
   resolving this data quality gap would improve model coverage by
   ~1.4%(1,386 additional customers scored).
